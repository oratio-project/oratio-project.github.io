ERP

http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Enterprise_resource_planning

ERP is a business management software—usually a suite of integrated applications—that a company can use to collect, store, manage and interpret data from many business activities, including:

  • Manufacturing planning
  • Marketing and sales
  • Inventory and shipping management
  • Accounting and Finance Analysis

Web based technology

  • The Perl Programming Language
  • Java
  • PostgreSQL
  • Firefox Mozilla
